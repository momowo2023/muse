Wisdom Teeth


I find myself grappling with a sentiment when it comes to wisdom teeth. From my perspective, wisdom teeth serve as intriguing souvenirs from our ancestors, traveling through space-time via our DNA. Though we exist in a different era than our ancestors, the genetic code acts as a kind of time machine, delivering these vestigial organs to us. I used different firing techniques which were developed at different times, to represent the wisdom teeth from different era.
